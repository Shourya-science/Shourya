About

I am a fourth-year undergraduate at the Institute of Chemical Technology (ICT), Mumbai, pursuing a B.Tech in Polymer Engineering & Technology with Honours alongside a Multidisciplinary Minor in Materials Science.

My academic interests lie at the intersection of Materials Science, polymer synthesis, adhesive science, and functional coatings. My current research, under Prof. Dr. S.T. Mhaske at the DPSE department, focuses on developing bio-derived, UV-curable pressure-sensitive adhesives with ultra-high optical clarity — an area bridging green chemistry, photopolymerisation, and optical materials.

Beyond research, I coordinate Vortex, ICT's annual technical festival, and have contributed to community development through the GJK Foundation. I am actively looking for PhD research opportunities in advanced polymer materials and sustainable functional systems.

Bio-based UV-curable Optical Clarity PSA

DPSE, ICT Mumbai · Supervisor: Prof. Dr. S.T. Mhaske
  • Synthesised a novel bio-derived UV-curable OCA-PSA with ultra-high optical clarity (>95% transmittance) via 100% solids, low-VOC photopolymerisation — eliminating thermal post-cure.
  • Established structure–property–performance relationships through DSC, FTIR, UV-Vis, and UTM (peel/tack) characterisation.
  • Enables optical bonding and display lamination applications with low environmental impact.

Chemical Recycling of PET from Acidic Polyester Effluent

Rangotsav 2026 · Advances in Polymers & Coatings · ICT Mumbai
  • Designed and presented a closed-loop valorisation strategy for pH ~2 polyester manufacturing effluent, proposing TPA-catalysed acid hydrolysis of PET waste to recover virgin-grade TPA (>98% yield) and ethylene glycol.
  • Proposed a complete downstream separation train (ceramic filtration → NaOH wash → H₂SO₄ precipitation) yielding pure TPA and oligomeric PET plasticiser, with feasibility assessed at 220°C and PET:H₂O = 1:8.
Feb 2026

Smart Heat-Managing Façade Paint for Thermal Buffering

Rangotsav 2026 · Advances in Polymers & Coatings · ICT Mumbai
  • Designed and presented a bio-based Stearic acid/Lauric acid phase change material microencapsulated in a SiO₂–polysiloxane shell (latent heat: 189.5 J/g, Tm: 39°C) for incorporation into façade paint.
  • Proposed 200-minute peak temperature delay and >90% enthalpy retention over 500 cycles — outperforming conventional cool-roof coatings on thermal storage, seasonal versatility, and aesthetics.
